Finished work | Rough work

I had a hard time with art in 2020, as I had a lot of mental issues dealing with work stress and the pandemic. I also cut down on screen time to balance out being stuck at home more, so I didn't get a lot of finished digital work done. I mostly sketched and doodled, some of which was done during downtime on the Surface I used at work. I had to use my phone camera for sketchbook pics, so apologies for the quality. More Guild Wars 2 fan art this year. Drew a bit of Pokemon stuff as my partner and I played SwSh a lot. I also got really into Sonic the Hedgehog, and experimented a bit with a Sonic inspired style. The last few drawings here were the first things I made with the iPad Pro 12.9" I got around the holidays.
