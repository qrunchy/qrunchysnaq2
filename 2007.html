I think this was by far the most prolific time of my life. I peaked in 2007. By then I had mostly moved on from Lion King fan art and got very interested in my own OCs again. I hung out on Suta-Raito a lot and got into oekaki for a bit.I also started being more uh, ~openly religious~ in my art. There's some Christian stuff here and there, but nothing too crazy. Mostly just like, crosses and shit. I am no longer religious so this is funny and kinda cringe to look back on.

I also became briefly interested in more abstract art, making weird colorful things in photoshop and playing around with fractal art.
